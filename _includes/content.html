This is your bag It feeds your brain while you eat. Teach, learn and enjoy!

Brown-bag is a short, thirty minutes, session which happens during your lunch break. It is your community-, pull-request- and open-source-driven unconference. The only thing which is predefined is the timeslots brown-bag will happen. Everything else is totally community-driven. If you feel we need additional slots or you would like to change how this uncoference works then get the discussion started. Eat your food or feed others, get your bag now!

I like to eat Subscribe to a brown-bag session, learn and enjoy!

Nothing simpler than that. Pick an already announced brown-bag session from the schedule, get yourself some nice and delicous food (hopefully in a brown-bag), make yourself comfortable in front of your screen or share it with your co-workers on a large screen and live-cast it into your office. Be sure to interact with your brown-bag friends all over the world by writing questions and comments in the screen-cast area and/or live tweet with the #brownbag hashtag. But don't just lean back in your seats. Get involved and feed others by hosting your brown-bag session!

I like to feed Your brown-bag session is just a pull-request away.

Open up a pull-request for an available slot, promote it, when it gets more vote than other pull-request for a given slot it gets merged and the slot is yours. Knock your audience out with your content! All you need is a good internet conncetion, a decent headset or microphone, an interesting topic which gets upvoted by the community, an hour on the brown-bag day and a little bit of courage. Don't worry, we are a friendly community! Host a brown-bag session now, teach and enjoy!
