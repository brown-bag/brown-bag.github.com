This is your bag It feeds your brain while you eat. Teach, learn and enjoy!

Brown-bag is a short, thirty minutes, session that happens during your lunch break. It is your community-, pull-request- and open-source-driven unconference. The only thing that is predefined are the timeslots brown-bag will happen. Everything else is totally community-driven. If you feel we need additional slots, or you would like to change how this unconference works then get the discussion started. Eat your food or feed others, get your bag now!
